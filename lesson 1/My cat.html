The story of my cat

Chapter 1. Tigra

My cat's name is Tigra because she was born in the year of a Tiger.

We met on a rainy day in the city center.

She ran across the road to dig in the sand on the opposite side of the street.

I ran after her and took her in my arms.

She was about the size of my palm, and my palm is small, so you can imagine how tiny she was 12 years ago.

She is now in her prime. You can see her latest photo here.
